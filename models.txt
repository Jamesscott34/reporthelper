USAGE NOTES:
============
- Premium models cost more but are most reliable
- Standard models offer good balance of cost/reliability
- Free models may have infrastructure issues
- System automatically tries models in order of preference
- If one model fails, it automatically switches to the next
- Legacy models are kept for backward compatibility

TO CHANGE MODELS:
=================
1. Update environment variables:
   BREAKDOWN_MODEL=openai/gpt-4o
   REVIEWER_MODEL=anthropic/claude-3.5-sonnet
   FINALIZER_MODEL=openai/gpt-4o-mini

2. Or update the model_config.py file directly

3. Restart the application after changes
